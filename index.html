Introduction

We would like to introduce ourselves as Manufacturers / Assemblers of Relay coils such as Wire wound and Tape wound, Switch Gear Coils, Transformers etc. We are in this field since 2004 as a preferred and major supplier for the following companies

  • Easun Reyrolle Limited, Hosur
  • S & S Power Switchgear Limited, Chennai
  • Megawin SwitchGear Private Limited, Salem
  • Phase Power Protection Limited, Canada
  • Gyro Power System, Hosur

We have the capacity of Manufacturing 1,00,000 + relay coils and Transformers in a year. Apart from this we do manufacture other assemblies like riveting, drilling etc on demand basis. Our Shop floor is spread across 1000sqft area and Manpower strength is around 9 members in all levels. We have strategic plans to expand our capacities in double from what we have now, which needs to expand our customer base also. In parallel we are in the process of expanding our customer base.
